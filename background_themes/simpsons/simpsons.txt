Design Rules (Very Important)

To look “Simpsons-authentic”, follow these rules:

No gradients
Colors are flat fills only.

Black outlines everywhere
Uniform stroke thickness.

High saturation
Colors are vivid and unapologetic.

Simple shading (optional)
When used, it’s usually one darker flat tone — no blending.

Typical Simpsons Color Combinations

Characters: #FFD90F skin + bold clothing colors

Sky: #70D1FE

Grass: #7ED957
